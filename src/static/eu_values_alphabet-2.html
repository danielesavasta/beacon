Notes — Entries marked * indicate a language appearing more than once. Letters A, H, N, Q, X, Z have no direct match among the six EU values in any of the 24 official EU languages; the words shown for those letters are kindred concepts from the broader European values tradition, chosen to complete the alphabet: A Αξιοπρέπεια (Greek, Dignity — kept from previous version); H Hodnota (Czech / Slovak, "Value"); N Naasteliefde (Dutch, "Love of one's neighbour"); Q Qualidade (Portuguese, "Quality"); X Xenofilia (Portuguese, "Love of the foreign / openness to others"); Z Zusammenhalt (German, "Cohesion / solidarity"). Within each script, cards are sorted alphabetically by their initial letter. All 24 official languages of the European Union are represented at least once.
