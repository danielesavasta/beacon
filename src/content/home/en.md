Beacon is a Blended Intensive Programme (BIP) bringing together design students and academics from nine partner universities across Europe and Latin America. Over an online phase and an in-person week in Palermo, participants explore picture language as a shared visual communication tool.

The project code is **BIP 2024-1-IT02-KA131-HED-000197121-2**, funded by the Erasmus+ programme.
