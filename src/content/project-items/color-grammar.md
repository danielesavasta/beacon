title: "Colour Grammar"
authors:
  - "Anna Wiśniewska"
  - "Luca Ferretti"
description: "An investigation into colour as syntax. The project proposes a grammar of hue, saturation and brightness that mirrors the structure of natural language: nouns rendered in primary hues, verbs in secondary tones, adjectives in tints and shades. Applied to the European Values word list, the result is a set of colour-coded visual sentences that can be read without text."
