description: "A comparative atlas mapping pictographic traditions across the nine partner countries. The project sources symbols from local visual cultures — road signs, folk patterns, currency, civic iconography — and tests their universality: which symbols travel across borders without explanation, and which ones fail? The atlas visualises gaps and overlaps as a network diagram."
